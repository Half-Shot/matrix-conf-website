Volunteer for the Conference

To keep the ticket price accessible for everyone and provide the best experience as possible, we need volunteers to lend a hand!

Contact us

Stage Host

  • Introducing speakers on stage, connecting the speaker to the audio/video crew and leading through the Q&A
  • max. 8 hours either on Thursday, Friday or Saturday
  • You will be hosting one stage throughout the day
This is a great way to be able to listen to many talks and meet some of the speakers while volunteering!

Build up of the event

  • Installing equipment in the morning of the first conference day
  • max. 4 hours on Thursday September 19
  • this tasks requires light physical work like caring boxes with merch
This task is perfect if you want to volunteer and still don’t miss a minute of the event!

Break down of the event

  • packing up after the event has ended
  • max. 4 hours on Sunday the 22nd in the afternoon
  • this tasks requires light physical work like caring boxes with merch
This task is perfect if you want to volunteer and still don’t miss a minute of the event!

Registration

  • Welcoming attendees, scanning tickets and handing out badges
  • max. 8 hours either on Thursday, Friday, Saturday or Sunday
Easy but very important task as you are the first point of contact for attendees and speakers once they arrive at the venue

Matrix.org Merch Booth

  • Handing out pre-ordered merch & selling merch
  • max. 8 hours either on Thursday, Friday or Saturday
Help us bring Matrix.org merch to people!

Accessibility Assistance

  • Helping people with accessibility needs navigate the conference
  • max. 8 hours either on Thursday, Friday, Saturday or Sunday
Make a real difference in our effort to have an event as inclusive as possible

Awareness Team

  • Enforcing the code of conduct and health policy
  • max. 8 hours either on Thursday, Friday, Saturday or Sunday
  • this tasks requires some sort of experience e.g with the enforcement of an code of conduct
Make a real difference in our effort to make the event as inclusive as possible

Volunteering grants you free access to the complete event, and a limited edition t-shirt and mug. We will create a schedule to make sure volunteers can enjoy the conference too. The more people lend a hand, the less work each one has to do!

If you are interested in making a difference, please email us at conference@foundation.matrix.org.
